Help

At the main photo display view: flick sideways to see more photos, tap the fullscreen button to hide the buttons, tap the "i" button to see more information about the photo.

When viewing the information about the photo you can tap the Flickr icon to view on Flickr or press and hold on the map to bring up the Google Map app in a new card.

As with most WebOS apps you can get back to the previous view by making the back gesture right-to-left on the blank area below the screen.

To customize the app, choose "Preferences" from the small menu at the top left of the screen.
