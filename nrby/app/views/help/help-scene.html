Help

At the main photo display view: Flick sideways to see more photos. Pinch out to zoom into the photo. Tap for information about the photo.

When viewing the information about the photo you can tap on the small icon to return to the main photo display view, or you can tap the "View on Flickr" button to bring up the photo on the Flickr web site.

As with most WebOS apps you can get back to the previous view by making the back gesture right-to-left on the blank area below the screen.

To customize the app, choose "Preferences" from the small menu at the top left of the screen.
